About Me

I am a creative problem-solver with 13+ years experience developing and implementing advanced analytical tools to uncover insights from complex data sets.


A theater techie at heart, I originally worked in the film and theater industry before I (surprisingly) discovered a passion for the brain and its inner workings. This led me to pursue a doctorate degree in Neuroscience, where I gained expertise in advanced research, experimentation, and analysis.


After many years in the lab, I am currently pivoting towards my next data-centric role, where I can bring my love for data and storytelling to a results-oriented data science and analytics team.
